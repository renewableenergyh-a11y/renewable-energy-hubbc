## Overview
This introductory module covers the basic principles of biomass energy, its role in renewable energy systems, and the fundamental concepts behind biomass-based electricity and heat generation.

- Agricultural residues and waste
- Forestry residues
- Dedicated energy crops
- Municipal and industrial waste

### Carbon Cycle and Energy

- Carbon neutrality considerations
- Combustion process
- Energy release and efficiency
