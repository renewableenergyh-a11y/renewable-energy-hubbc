# Module 4: Wind Resource Assessment & Site Selection

## Overview
This advanced module covers the technical assessment of wind resources and selection of optimal project sites.

## Wind Resource Evaluation

### Wind Measurement
- Anemometer types and placement
- Measurement period requirements
- Data quality and validation
- Uncertainty analysis

### Wind Speed Analysis
- Wind speed distribution (Weibull)
- Frequency distribution analysis
- Mean wind speed calculation
- Seasonal variations

### Wind Shear and Turbulence
- Vertical wind shear
- Surface roughness effects
- Turbulence intensity measurement
- Wind modeling

## Site Selection Criteria

### Geographic Factors
- Latitude and elevation
- Proximity to coast vs. inland
- Terrain and topography
- Local climate patterns

### Resource Potential
- Annual energy production (AEP) estimation
- Capacity factor assessment
- Wind rose analysis
- Exclusion zones

### Environmental and Social Factors
- Environmental sensitivity areas
- Proximity to habitats
- Community acceptance
- Land use compatibility

## Assessment Tools and Methods

### Modeling Software
- WAsP and similar tools
- Micrositing analysis
- Numerical weather prediction
- Uncertainty quantification

### Economic Considerations
- Resource adequacy for profitability
- Minimum wind speed thresholds
- Economic exclusion criteria

## Learning Outcomes
Upon completion, you will be able to:
- Conduct wind resource assessment
- Evaluate site suitability
- Interpret wind resource data
- Assess project viability
- Manage assessment uncertainties
