# Module 2: Wind Turbine Components

## Overview
This module covers the essential components that make up a modern wind turbine and their functions in converting wind energy into electricity.

## Key Components

### Rotor Blades
- Aerodynamic design principles
- Materials and construction
- Blade pitch and yaw mechanisms
- Performance optimization

### Nacelle
- Gearbox and drivetrain
- Generator types and specifications
- Control systems
- Structural design

### Tower and Foundation
- Tower materials and design
- Foundation types
- Structural analysis
- Maintenance access

### Electrical Components
- Transformer systems
- Power conversion equipment
- Grid connection devices
- Safety systems

## Learning Outcomes
By the end of this module, you will understand:
- The function of each major turbine component
- How components work together as a system
- Material properties and design considerations
- Maintenance requirements for each component
